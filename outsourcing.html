How you engage us

Three flexible ways to work together

Pick the model that fits your practice — switch or combine as your needs change. No long lock-in, and every job runs through a senior reviewer before it reaches you (maker–checker).

Model 01

Dedicated resource

Your own offshore accountant/bookkeeper working only for your firm — priced per person, per month. Best for steady, ongoing volume.

Model 02

Managed / per-job

Hand us whole jobs (a BAS, a tax return, a set of financials) on a white-label basis. You get the result without managing a person.

Model 03

Overflow hours

Pre-purchased block hours you draw down during BAS quarters and EOFY peaks — capacity exactly when you need it.
