How outsourcing works

We slot into your practice — not the other way around

You stay the registered agent and the face to your clients. We're the capacity behind you, working to your standards in your software.

1

Scope & fit

We discuss the work, volumes and turnaround you need, and agree on a trial.

2

Secure access

You grant controlled access to your XPM, Xero and FYI Docs under your firm's policies.

3

Setup & SOPs

We configure recurring jobs, templates and checklists so work runs the same way every time.

4

We process

Our team completes jobs to your standards and flags anything that needs a decision.

5

You review & sign

Your firm reviews, approves and lodges — you retain professional responsibility throughout.

How you engage us

Three flexible ways to work together

Pick the model that fits your practice — switch or combine as your needs change. No long lock-in, and every job runs through a senior reviewer before it reaches you (maker–checker).

Model 01

Dedicated resource

Your own offshore accountant/bookkeeper working only for your firm — priced per person, per month. Best for steady, ongoing volume.

Model 02

Managed / per-job

Hand us whole jobs (a BAS, a tax return, a set of financials) on a white-label basis. You get the result without managing a person.

Model 03

Overflow hours

Pre-purchased block hours you draw down during BAS quarters and EOFY peaks — capacity exactly when you need it.

Questions

What practices usually ask

Do you contact our clients?

No. We operate entirely behind the scenes. All client communication stays with your firm — we're white-label by design.

Who is responsible for lodgement and sign-off?

Your practice. You remain the registered tax/BAS agent, you review the work, and you lodge. We prepare the work to your standards for your approval.

Which software do you work in?

We work natively in Xero Practice Manager (XPM), Xero and FYI Docs, and can adapt to your existing workflows and templates.

How is our data kept secure?

We use controlled, role-based access to your systems, confidentiality agreements, and careful data-handling practices. We're happy to align with your firm's security policy.

How does pricing work?

Pricing depends on the work type, volume and turnaround. We keep it simple and competitive — contact us for a quote tailored to your practice.

Can you also build our website and handle SEO?

Yes — that's our Digitech division. We can manage your domain, website, hosting and search visibility. See Digitech.
